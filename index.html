Who we are

Your premier island performance collective

The Golden Hour Collective is a Maui-based group built around warm harmonies and songs everyone knows by heart.

We tailor our sound to fit your vision providing options from soulful acoustic duo to a dynamic quartet to a full ensemble with horns. We are excited to make your ideas come to life.
